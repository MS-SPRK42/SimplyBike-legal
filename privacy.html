SimplyBike Privacy Policy

SimplyBike is designed to be a simple, low-pressure riding companion.

What we collect

• Email address (for account access)
• Ride data (duration, intervals, optional distance, optional feedback)
• Equipment settings (resistance range)

What we do with it

• Save your rides so you can view your progress
• Maintain your account and preferences
• Improve app reliability and performance

What we do NOT do

• We do not sell your data
• We do not use your data for advertising
• We do not track you across other apps or services

Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud).

Your control

You can delete your data at any time by:

• Using the delete account option within the app
• Contacting support at support@sprk42.com

Updates

This policy may be updated as the product evolves.
